SYMPATHY

EXPRESSIVE FLORAL GESTURES

When you need a floral gesture to express your feelings during emotional times, our team of highly trained florists are well place to advise you on the best designs with a seasonal choice of flowers. We understand that sending funeral or sympathy flowers can be very difficult and we are here to help you every step of the way to ensure that perfect floral arrangement. Here at Sweetpea Florist, we pride ourselves on creating designs that show your loved ones that your thoughts are with them during difficult times.

We have over 20 years of experience at creating funeral and sympathy flowers so whether you are looking to create traditional or contemporary designs, our team of experienced florists can help you choose the most fitting arrangements.

Sweetpea Florist guarantees delivery on time and we make arrangements with funeral directors to ensure that your beautiful, fresh, handmade arrangements arrive at the right place, and on time. If you wish to send them elsewhere - such as the residence, crematorium or church; that can also be arranged.

For more information on our sympathy and funeral arrangements please visit us in-store at 55a South End Rd NW3 2QB, email us at info@thesweetpeaflorist.com or call Sweetpea Florist on +44 (0) 207 431 7767.

ABOUT US

FLORISTRY FROM THE HEART OF HAMPSTEAD

Sweet Pea Florist is located close to the stunning surroundings of Hampstead Heath, providing a vast variety of floral arrangements. Our team of highly skilled, talented and creative florists approach each project with great attention to detail and enthusiasm. We are committed to ensuring the final product is everything you imagined and more.

After 15 years of working in the floral industry we decided to open our own family run florist.
